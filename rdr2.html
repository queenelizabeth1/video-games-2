Red Dead Redemption II



Red Dead Redemption 2 is an action adventure game released for Playstation and Xbox in 2018, and PC in 2019. It is a prequel to Red Dead Redemption. The story takes place in the late 1800s and follows Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde Gang, a cowboy gang coping with the near end of the wild West. A major theme of the story is family, as the gang is always looking out for each other- or was looking out for each other. As the gang faces worse and worse fate from several enemies such as the Pinkertons and other cowboy gangs, members become more and more selfish until gradually, the gang is broken. In addition to a complex long story, the game is open world and beautifully detailed, with several gorgeous locations such as snowy mountains, humid swamps, vast lakes, and an industrialized city. With buildings such as hotels, stores, bars, and hair salons, several NPCs and side quests, many fashion options as well as abilities to go hunting or fishing, a player of this game can really feel like a cowboy in the late 1800s.

Gameplay

The player plays as Arthur Morgan and can choose to either be in first person or third person mode. As the game is open world, the player is free to go wherever they want. They can travel by foot, horse, or wagon. A map indicates where quests are or where a player is wanted. The player can go to several leisure outings such as hotels, bars, restaurants, hair salons, and theaters. It is possible to actually play games such as poker and dominoes. In addition, the player can go hunting or fishing in the wilderness. Shops allow the player to purchase guns and clothes from a wide variety of choices and to stack up on provisions. To well mimic reality, Arthur and his horse have health and stamina. These can be refreshed by sleep or food.

Story

In addition to detailed gameplay, Red Dead Redemption II offers an intricate and touching plot. The story begins with the Dutch Van Der Linde outlaw gang struggling to survive in the mountains, where they were forced to escape after a robbery in Blackwater went horribly wrong. The crew make their way to Horshoe Overlook where they hide out and recuperate after the awful few months they had lived through. The leader of the gang, Dutch, wants to get back in the outlaw game so that his gang can leave Horshoe Overlook and return West. Dutch's tactics do the opposite, as the gang is found by the Pinkerton police, and the members are forced to run away again. They head to a swamp town called Lemoyne. Dutch notices a humongous feud between the Gray and Braithwait families, and he attempts to con the two families to retrieve much money for the gang. However, the families find out about this trick, and they end up shooting down the gang. Once more, the gang is forced to escape, and they look for money opportunities in Saint Denis. Saint Denis is an urbanized city and the opposite of the wild free land the gang desires. Gang members begin to realize the Age of Cowboys is dying. Dutch, however, refuses to give up hope, and he becomes more and more careless with his plans, desperate for the gang to make its big break again. Dutch finds a lucrative bank robbing opportunity for the gang, but somehow, the police show up once again. The police shoot several gang members and arrest many others. It is clear to the gang that there is a mole in the gang. Arthur, Dutch, and Micah escape on a boat and are stranded on Guarma, an island on the Caribbean. They manage to return and reunite with the gang, or at least the members who are still alive. Several gang members have given up hope of ever finding the Wild West and have left. Meanwhile, Dutch's plans become crazier and crazier, as he leaves Arthur,who is like a son to him, to die and takes advantage of the heavily discriminated indigenous people. Later, Arthur learns that Micah has been working with the Pinkertons to betray the gang. Arthur confronts Micah, and the gang splits into those supporting Micah and those supporting Arthur. But eventually, the Pinkertons come after the gang, and in the last event of the story, Arthur and John run away from the Pinkertons and the betraying gang members. After a bloody fight with Micah, Arthur falls to the ground and dies after witnessing a beautiful sunset. In the epilogue, the player plays as John Marston who builds a farm, marries Abigail, and avenges Arthur by finding Micah and shooting him.
